Blue Boat Log

Blue Boat Log is the Simple Way of logging and sharing your sailing trips with everyone. Just track your trip, add a description and/or image and share it with your family, friends, followers and other app users. No internet connection is required to track. Just upload the data whenever you have a signal again. Now available on Google Play and the App Store!

ReeContact

With a busy life and career, the idea of a Contact Manager that would make it easier for us to stay in touch with people was an easy one. So we built ReeContact, a Contact Manager that helps you to better stay in touch with the people you care about. It offers a fine-grained priority system as well as extensive note-taking capabilities make things easier for you even with a busy life.

Shaving Buddy

Originally built for Android in 2014, this project has managed to gain a lot of traction in the shaving enthusiast community by allowing people to track their collection, log and evaluate shaves and share them with the community. More recently, this project has been upgraded to reflect our extended knowledge and will soon be available not only for Android, but also iOS!
